Welcome to Next Nature

The first NFT series about nature, that gives back to ecosystems around the world!

The Project

The Next Nature project and its 365 unique unique NFT's are made with a intent to support the global help for preserving and nourishing the nature. This is being done by donating 10% of each and every NFT purchase to a variety of organizations which help to either preserve or re-build certain eco systems which are necessary for the wellbeing of all of us.

Our partners

Idea behind the project

The Next Nature team is composed of people thriving with initiatives to help spread awareness about the current state of ecosystems around the world, what is wrong with the current way we as a society are managing these ecosystems and how we as individuals can help. Together with our love for crypto, developing, art and nature, we thought of creating a NFT to merge all this together and help as much as we can by providing part of the profits from the collection to organizations whose mission is to help the nature.

Minting

Next Nature project will make 365 unique NFT's available to the public on release date at a cost of 0.2 Ethereum. Targeted release date as of now is 30th of January. With a pre-sale on 28th of January for whitelisted members with the capacity of 250 NFT's in the pre-sale.

Roadmap

Introduction [Q4 2021]

Introduce our accounts and goals on social media platforms (Twitter & Dsicord).
Introduce the project and grow the community via Discord and Twitter by sharing goals of the project and collection.

WhitelistT [Q1 2022]

Various activity based competitions will be held mainly on our Discord server for members to be able to access our whitelist.
Further on, whitelisted members will be given various perks including giveaway entries for other activities and goodies within the server and will be granted early access to the minting process before the official release.

Website[Q1 2022]

This website will serve as a improved and much more visual representation of our project, goals and processes of distribution and minting.
It will also serve as a gateway, down the road to the official minting event once our team is ready to launch the whole NFT collection.

Mint Event [Q1 2022]

The mint event will consist of 365 unique NFT's of the Next Nature project featuring various nature based nft artworks, set up on a rarity scale.
The rarity scale will also be released before the mint event so that all the members of the community will have a clear understanding of how our rarity system works.

Secondary Markets [Q1 2022]

As a part of our mission all royalties from secondary markets, which would in case of other projects be forwarded towards the project owners will be in our case donated to our partners.

Collaboration [Q1 2022]

Our aim is to manage various collaborations with nature oragnisations and foundations on repetetive basis.
Another big aim of the project in relation to collaborations is to establish connections with other brands and influencers with the same goals as ours, to help save the ecosystems.

Creation of Non-Profit Organisation[Q2 2022]

The major goal of this oragnisation will be to help with recovery of damaged ecosystems around the globe.
Recruit voulanteers for various works and activities we will host to help with local ecosystem preservation.
